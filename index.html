Our Story

we M/s. Raise Garments , from tirupur (tamilnadu) one of the leading manufacture of all kind of garments. Our well established infrastructure helps us to manufacture superior quality range of Knitted Garments and Knitted Fabrics. These are manufactured using optimum grade raw material procured from the trusted vendors of the market and latest machines in order to meet the international quality standards. Our offered garments are manufactured by our one of the group company named M/s. Raise Garments using advanced technology and high quality basic material. Further, our clients can avail our entire product range in customized specifications as per their requirements at industry leading prices. Our ethical business practices, transparent business dealings, client centric approach and timely delivery have enabled us to attain the distinct position in the market.
